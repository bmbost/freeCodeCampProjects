A Tribute to Nintendo

Nintendo logo prior to 2006

Nintendo's Consoles in Order of Release:

  • 1977: Color TV-Game
  • 1980: Game & Watch (Produced until the 1990s)
  • 1983: Famicom / Nintendo Entertainment System (NES, 1985 in U.S.)
  • 1989: GameBoy
  • 1990: Super Famicom / Super NES (1991 U.S.)
  • 1996: Nintendo 64, GameBoy Pocket
  • 1998: GameBoy Light
  • 2001: GameCube, GameBoy Advance
  • 2003: GameBoy Advance SP
  • 2004: DS
  • 2005: GameBoy Micro
  • 2006: Wii, DS Lite
  • 2008: DSi (Japan, worldwide 2009)
  • 2009: DSi XL (Japan, worldwide 2010)
  • 2011: 3DS
  • 2012: Wii U, 3DS XL
  • 2013: 2DS (Most of the world, Japan release 2016)
  • 2014: New 3DS and 3DS XL(Japan, U.S. release 2015)
  • 2017: Switch (releases in March)
